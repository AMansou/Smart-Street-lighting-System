Smart street lighting

Make Streets Safer. Efficient. Smarter.

Start managing lights

LED Lighting

 The main advantages of LEDs over incandescent lamps are that they are a lot more energy efficient, have better lighting quality, last longer and can utilize PWM.

Light detection

Light sensors will be utilized to sense the surrounding light in order to decide when lumination and how much lumination is needed. as well as to detect faulty lights.

Motion detection

the system can further improve its efficiency by only intensifying the light when motion is detected.

Library

Each smart street light forms a node in an interconnected network to keep the road ahead of you well lit using the ZigBee protocol.
